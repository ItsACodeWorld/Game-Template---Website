I will be creating a zombie survival game where users can interact with one another, trading supplies and other things I haven't thought up yet.
There will be quests the player can fulfill to gain supplies and experience. These quests take energy to do and may do damage to the player if they fail
With supplies, the user can build weapons, armor, or medicine
Weapons increase damage, Armor increases defense
Medicine can increase energy, hp, or other buffs

Players can also fight one another, doing damage, stealing supplies, and gaining exp

The purpose is to gain a high level and become the ultimate zombie survivalist


There may be more features added if I think of anything fun
